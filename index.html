🔎 Launch AML-related Google searches for a list of subjects.

Created by Travis Birch, 2022
** Important: Allow pop ups!! **
Instructions:
  1. Enter your subjects' names in the box. You can add multiple names, but they must be separated by either commas or new lines. (Pasting from a spreadsheet should work.)
  2. Click "Create links" to generate search buttons.
  3. Click each button to open custom searches.
  4. Click "Clear all" to start over.
